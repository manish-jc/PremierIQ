# Erling Haaland

## Basic Information

- Full Name: Erling Braut Haaland
- Club: Manchester City
- Position: Striker
- Nationality: Norway

## Playing Style

Erling Haaland is a powerful striker known for his exceptional finishing, intelligent movement, pace, and physical strength. He excels at making runs behind defenders and scoring inside the penalty area.

## Strengths

- Clinical Finishing
- Pace
- Positioning
- Strength
- Aerial Ability

## Achievements

- Premier League Champion
- UEFA Champions League Winner
- Premier League Golden Boot

## Weaknesses

- Limited involvement in build-up play
- Can be less effective against deep defensive blocks
