# Mohamed Salah

## Basic Information

- Full Name: Mohamed Salah Hamed Mahrous Ghaly
- Club: Liverpool
- Position: Right Winger
- Nationality: Egypt

## Playing Style

Mohamed Salah is a quick and skillful winger known for his pace, dribbling, finishing, and intelligent movement. He frequently cuts inside from the right wing to create scoring opportunities and is one of the Premier League's most consistent attacking players.

## Strengths

- Finishing
- Dribbling
- Pace
- Ball Control
- Off-the-ball Movement
- Chance Creation

## Achievements

- Premier League Champion
- UEFA Champions League Winner
- Premier League Golden Boot Winner
- FIFA Club World Cup Winner
- Multiple Liverpool Player of the Season Awards

## Weaknesses

- Can be tightly marked by defensive teams
- Aerial ability is not one of his strongest attributes
